Sessions

We run three regular sessions a week. Visitors are most welcome. Our hosted sessions on Monday and Tuesday mean we’ll find you a partner if you come alone. For Thursday evening if you would like a partner please contact the club.

Monday afternoon - Open to all grades. Please be seated by 1:20pm - Play to commence 1:30pm. No partner required. Table money $5.

Tuesday evening - Informal Junior Improvement. Relaxed. Please be seated by 6:20 pm - Play to commence 6:30pm. About 15 hands, finishing before 9:30 pm. No partner required. Table money $3.

Thursday evening - Open to all grades. Please be seated by 6:50pm - Play to commence 7:00pm. Table money $5.

Lessons

Courses for beginners are held once a year. If you are interested in learning, email the club (keribridge@xtra.co.nz) and we will contact you before they start.

Short improvement sessions are held before play on Tuesday evening.

2023 Lessons begin 16th April at 7pm and run for 10 weeks.
